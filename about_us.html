Our Story

Driving digital transformation through innovation, expertise, and a commitment to excellence

Our Journey

2010

Company Founded

Sybyl was established with a team of 5 professionals focused on IT infrastructure solutions, operating from a small office in Nairobi.

2014

First Major Enterprise Client

Secured our first banking sector client, marking our entry into financial technology solutions and establishing our reputation in the industry.

2017

Regional Expansion

Expanded operations to Tanzania and Uganda, establishing regional offices and building a pan-East African presence.

2020

Digital Transformation Focus

Launched our dedicated digital transformation practice to help businesses adapt to new technologies in the post-pandemic world.

2023

100+ Employees

Grew to over 100 technology professionals serving clients across East Africa with comprehensive enterprise solutions.

Our Mission

To empower businesses through innovative technology solutions that drive growth, efficiency, and competitive advantage in the digital age. We combine global expertise with local understanding to deliver transformative results.

Our Vision

To be the leading digital transformation partner for enterprises in Africa, recognized for our technical excellence, innovative approach, and commitment to client success. We aim to bridge the technology gap in emerging markets.
